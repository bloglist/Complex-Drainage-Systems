Complex Drainage Systems

Addressing drainage issues at the onset can save you from the headache of costly repairs down the line. Pacific Land Preservation specializes in mitigating these problems before they escalate, particularly when water accumulates in the lower-lying sections of your property, posing a risk to structural integrity. To combat such challenges, we deploy an efficient and minimally invasive solution: the installation of French drains. This tailored system is designed to divert water away from critical areas, such as near your home's foundation, ensuring that accumulation and potential damage are averted.

A French drain system is strategically placed in the most susceptible areas of your lawn to facilitate proper water drainage, preventing the possibility of water pooling and the subsequent risks it poses to your property. The installation process is streamlined to avoid significant disruption, requiring minimal excavation time. This approach not only safeguards your basement or foundation from unwanted water ingress but also mitigates the risk of developing a damp or flooded basement or crawl space, which can lead to extensive and expensive remediation efforts.
